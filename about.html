About:

After a break of nearly a decade, this traditional artist took up sketching again as a means to keep busy during slack moments at work. Eventually, someone online took an interest in the artist's skills and the artist became determined to improve. Since that clandestine day, the artist has used many types of media as seen by the table below and the frequency of use. Interesting Fact: The artist's personal gallery has over 3k images.
Media Used and Frequency of Use
Media Usage
Pen & Ink Frequent
Watercolor Frequent
Arcylic Seldom
Gouache Frequent

Pastel Seldom
