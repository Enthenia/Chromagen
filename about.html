    • About Chromagen:
    After a break of nearly a decade, this traditional artist took up sketching again as a means to keep busy during slack moments at work. Eventually, someone online took an interest in the artist's skills and the artist became determined to improve. Since that clandestine day, the artist has used many types of media as seen by the table below and the frequency of use. As a note of interest, the artist's personal gallery has over 3k images.
    Media Used and Frequency of Use
    Media Usage
    Pen and Ink Frequent
    Watercolor Frequent
    Arcylic Seldom
    Gouache Frequent
    Pencil Frequent
    Pastel Seldom
