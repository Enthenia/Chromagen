Introduction:


Chromagen rose from a concept brought about by an interest in traditional media, challenges, and a desire to keep busy. Always striving to do something unique, Chromagen is a site where an independant artist can showcase work and where people interested in traditional media can view and appreciate such methods.

About Chromagen:

Chromagen is a design source specializing in traditional media including:

  • Pen & Ink
  • Watercolor
  • Gouache
  • Pencils
